## README
This repo is part of my Masters Project at the University of Gibraltar. It involved designing and carry out a vessel- and land-based systematic distance sampling survey
within the British Gibraltar Territorial Waters (BGTW) to estimate the Distribution and Abundance of Cetaceans within them. 

Within [data](/data), all data especially a detailed shapefile of the British Gibraltar Territorial Waters and the Bay of Algeciras/Gibraltar can be found.

### Method
In order to estimate the relative a bundance and distribution of cetaceans within the BGTW I used a systematic distance sampling approach. I used a equal spaced zigzag design to acieve an equal coverage of the survey area and minimise travel time between transect legs and off-effort time.
Because the University's boat was not able to be used I had to switch from vessel-based surveys to land-based surveys. A detailed approach as well as a map of the Survey Area can be found in here.
This repo is part of the survey design, the data analysis and data visualisation. It can be used as a tool to comprehend the steps that need to go into the survey design. 

#### Land-based Distance Sampling
Following methods established by Buckland et al. land-based point sampling was adapted to be used in along the Gibraltar coast. Three spots were selected and cover a larger portion of the BGTW.
